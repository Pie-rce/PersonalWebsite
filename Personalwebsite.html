We are here to help you understand your place in the world of pianists. Based on your technique and skill, you'll be able to assess your progress more easily. You'll also find techniques that will help you play piano more effortlessly and with clarity. But, keep in mind, it won’t always be easy. Think of it like this...

If you were an explorer (and you are, in the world of piano), trying to find your way might feel like only 10% of your crew will make it back. That crew is your desire to play piano.

On this homepage, you’ll find the fundamentals and principles laid out at their core. These will help you improve as quickly as possible, but more importantly, they’ll explain *why* you should practice them to see progress.
