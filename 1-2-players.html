Level: 1-2 Players

Welcome to the 1-2 Player Level

A 1-2 player should be able to play all 12 major scales, with both hands synchronized, one finger at a time. They should be able to play it up and down the piano. they should also be able to play each of them once through with no mistakes. They should also be able to play the following songs with both hands.

  • Ode to Joy
  • Happy birthday
  • Heart and Soul (both hand parts)
  • And Twinkle twinle little star

This would demonstrate the proper technique performance for a beginner. A beginner that has the amount of technique and skill to show they are ready to start becoming more than a intermediate student.
